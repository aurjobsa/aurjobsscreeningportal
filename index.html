About Aurjobs AI

Aurjobs AI is a state-of-the-art, AI-powered recruitment platform designed to help businesses streamline their hiring processes and improve recruitment outcomes. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and natural language processing, we help you match the most qualified candidates to job roles with precision.

Aurjobs AI is Founder by Keshav Agarwal and Currently Serves as CEO of Aurjobs.

The platform automates manual tasks like resume screening, skill matching, and candidate ranking, enabling your HR team to save valuable time and focus on the best-fit candidates. Whether you're a startup or a large enterprise, Aurjobs AI adapts to your recruitment needs.

At Aurjobs AI, we are committed to a bias-free hiring process that ensures fairness and diversity in your recruitment pipeline. Our platform integrates seamlessly with existing Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), allowing you to enhance your current recruitment workflow with powerful AI-driven tools.

How Aurjobs Screening AI Works

Step 1: Upload Job Description

Input your job description and let AI analyze the required skills and qualifications.

Step 2: Submit Candidates’ Resumes

Candidates upload their resumes directly to the platform or via integrations.

Step 3: AI-Driven Analysis & Ranking

AI analyzes resumes, ranks candidates, and presents insights to help you decide quickly.

Step 4: Shortlist & Interview

Review the shortlist of top candidates and schedule interviews seamlessly.
